Cheesy Ramen Noodles recipe

Description

Melting cheese in ramen makes for an extremely simple and delicious spin on typical ramen noodles that can be easily made in the college environment.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water.
  • 1 (3 ounce) package any flavour ramen noodles
  • 1 slice American cheese

Step by step guide on how to create the world's best tasting cheesy ramen noodles.

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring water to boil in a saucepan. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender, about 2 minutes.
  3. Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet and cheese until well blended.
