Lasagna recipe

Description

Making a lasagna may be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. Here is a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions on how to make the world's best tasting lasagna.

Lasagna ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
    • >
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 28 ounce can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 6.5 ounce canned tomato sauce
  • 2 6 ounce cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Step-by-Step guide on making a lasagna

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlin in a Dutch oven over medeium heat until well-browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmier, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occassionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoons of salt.
  6. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  7. To assemble, spread the 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinke with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray to make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
